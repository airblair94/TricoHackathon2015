Controlled Transitions & Standing Balance

Vinyasa Flow with Jo Tastula

A playful class exploring the biomechanics of balance in various standing and asymmetrical one legged poses. Paying special attention to the vertical line of gravity can bring you into a deeper state of concentration and meditation as you move towards stillness. Explore new movements in poses such as gate pose and extended head to knee. Props Suggested: A blanket, block and a strap.


Practice Focus: Standing Balance

Level 2-3 60 min

Mulabandha - A Practice to Activate and Stabilize Mind and Energy

Hatha with Rod Stryker

The root lock, one of the three major locks or bandhas, simultaneously grounds and stabilizes, while also activating dormant energy. Through a series of accessible postures you experience how to practice and develop this vital technique. This very mindful practice starts slowly, then gradually builds in intensity. This is a practice that will physically enliven the pelvic area and energetically lead to a greater sense of embodiment and mental clarity. Prop Suggested: One blanket.


Level 2-3 45 min

Yoga for Rock Climbers

Vinyasa Flow with Taylor Harkness

Rock climbing is an adventurous, adrenaline pounding, very challenging sport. As good as it is for the soul to be out in nature and for the mind to overcome fears and challenges, it can give the body a real beating. This practice will work on core strength and balance (essential components for climbing) and then release all of the tight spots climbers run into: forearms, wrists, shoulders, and hips. Practice yoga and 'climb on'. Props Suggested: Two blocks and a bolster.


Practice Focus: Cross Train

Level 1-2 30 min

Beginning Yoga: Build Strength & Flexibility

Ashtanga with Mary Taylor

This class is for those who are new to yoga and are looking for a practice that will build strength and flexibility over time. Learn the building blocks of the Ashtanga system and how to move in conjunction with the breath and gaze in a flowing sequence of postures with variations offered where appropriate. Postures are drawn from the Primary Series of Ashtanga. Props Needed: One block, blanket and strap.


Practice Focus: Tutorials

Hatha with Tara Judelle

This 10 minute alignment tutorial focuses on the anatomical strategy for aligning the neck and shoulders to help relieve deep pain and concludes with some easeful postures to help set the pattern in place.

Iyengar with Marla Apt

A couple of restorative asanas to calm the nervous system and prepare for pranayama. Props Needed: Three blankets, a strap and a block.
